# PHANTOM GRID — HANDOFF PROTOCOLS

Defines how the three personas pass work to each other. Every handoff has a sender, a receiver, a format, and a trigger.

---

## HYDRO THEORY → WAVEJUMPER
### Trigger: signing approved, artwork needed

HYDRO THEORY passes to WAVEJUMPER:

1. **The Signal** — one paragraph from `anr-decision.md`: what this music transmits
2. **Lineage position** — who the conversation partner is in the Detroit canon
3. **Three words** — the mood HYDRO THEORY hears in the music
4. **Any artist-supplied references** — visuals the artist has referenced or used before
5. **Format constraints** — single / EP / LP, any known platform requirements

WAVEJUMPER takes this and opens `artwork-brief.md`. She does not ask for more until the brief is drafted — she works from what she has and flags gaps explicitly.

---

## HYDRO THEORY → STORM SURGE
### Trigger: signing approved, promo arc needed

HYDRO THEORY passes to STORM SURGE:

1. **Artist profile** — who this is, where they're from, release history
2. **The Signal** — same one paragraph from `anr-decision.md`
3. **What the label is saying by signing this artist** — the implicit statement
4. **Timeline** — known dates or constraints
5. **What to avoid** — anything about the artist or release that should not be foregrounded publicly

STORM SURGE takes this and opens `promo-arc.md`. She does not invent context — she works from the handoff and asks if essential information is missing.

---

## WAVEJUMPER → STORM SURGE
### Trigger: artwork approved, social assets needed

WAVEJUMPER passes to STORM SURGE:

1. **Final artwork file reference** — what exists and in what formats
2. **Visual concept summary** — one paragraph: what the artwork does, what it communicates
3. **Color dominant** — which surface tiers and accent use are present
4. **What the artwork cannot carry alone** — what the copy needs to add or frame

STORM SURGE does not interpret the artwork freely — she uses WAVEJUMPER's concept summary as the frame for all copy.

---

## STORM SURGE → WAVEJUMPER
### Trigger: post concept needs a new visual

STORM SURGE passes to WAVEJUMPER:

1. **Post function** — ANNOUNCE / ESTABLISH / DEEPEN / ATMOSPHERE
2. **Copy draft** — full caption text already written
3. **What the visual must do** — specific visual requirement, not mood words
4. **Placement context** — where in the feed does this sit, what comes before and after
5. **Constraints** — size, format, timing

WAVEJUMPER does not produce a visual without a written function. "Make something atmospheric" is not a brief.

---

## ALL → VOID
### Trigger: any output ready for approval

All outputs are proposals. The handoff to Void is:

1. **What was produced** — the deliverable
2. **Why** — the reasoning behind every significant decision
3. **What requires a decision** — explicit list of open questions or choices Void must make
4. **What can proceed without input** — to avoid unnecessary back-and-forth

Void approves, modifies, or rejects. No persona treats silence as approval.
